Problem Scenario :

There is a growing need for an intelligent healthcare chatbot that can provide personalized medical advice and assistance to individuals at any time of the day or night.

With increasing demand for healthcare services and a shortage of doctors, it is often difficult for patients to access timely medical advice and support.

In addition, many people hesitate to seek medical help because of the stigma associated with certain medical conditions or the difficulty of going to a doctor's office.
